About Naawi-Oodena

Our Vision

Naawi-Oodena means "Center of the Heart and Community" in Anishinaabe. It's a major new urban development that brings together Indigenous traditions and modern city planning. This 160 acre site is being built through a special partnership between Treaty One Nation and the Canada Lands Company.

The goal is to build a beautiful and welcoming community that respects Indigenous culture and provides modern living. Naawi-Oodena will show how Indigenous-led projects can help shape the cities of the future.

Our Partners

This groundbreaking project is made possible through collaboration between:

  • Treaty One Nation: Representing seven Anishinaabe First Nations in southern Manitoba
  • Canada Lands Company: A federal Crown corporation with expertise in transformative property development
  • Local Indigenous Communities: Ensuring cultural authenticity and community benefits
